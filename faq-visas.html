Faq’s

Settleu provides comprehensive assistance with visa processes, relocation planning, and integration into your new environment in the EU.

Settleu partners with certified immigration consultants and legal experts to ensure that all visa applications and relocation plans comply with current EU regulations.

Settleu provides assistance for work visas, student visas, family reunification visas, and special permits like the EU Blue Card and digital nomad visas.

Settleu offers post-visa services, including finding housing, enrolling in local services, and navigating tax and healthcare systems in your destination country.

Settleu uses advanced encryption and strict data management protocols to ensure the safety and confidentiality of user information.

Visa applications are often rejected due to incomplete documentation, insufficient financial proof, or failure to meet eligibility requirements. Settleu ensures all submissions are accurate and complete.

Entrepreneurs can apply for start-up or investment visas offered by countries like Germany, Spain, and France. These visas often require a robust business plan and proof of investment.

Settleu provides expert guidance for reapplying after a rejection or extending an existing visa, including document preparation and submission support.

Spain, Portugal, and Estonia lead in offering attractive digital nomad visas with flexible income proof requirements and tax incentives for remote workers.

Settleu works with immigration experts and monitors official government updates to ensure all guidance is accurate and up-to-date.
